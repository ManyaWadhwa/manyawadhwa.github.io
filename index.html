Manya Wadhwa

I am currently a PhD student at the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning (TAUR) group, at the University of Texas at Austin, led by Professor Greg Durrett.

Previously, I was an Associate Research Engineer with the R&D team at Goldman Sachs for three years. This team was led by Vijay Saraswat. I also had the opportunity to work with great mentors like Johannes Hoffart and Luciano Del Corro. My work involved using Natural Language Understanding techniques in the space of Finance. Our focus main focus was Document Understanding. I also dabbled a little in Dialogue Understanding. Over the course of three I worked on a range of NLP tasks I worked on - Information Extraction, Entity Linking, Question Answering and Slot Filling.

I completed my Master's in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University where I worked with Professor Mark Dredze in an intersection of NLP and Regulatory Document Understanding.

I did my undergrad from IIIT-Delhi where I majored in Computer Science.

Beyond the research world, I like to volunteer my free time and work with non-profit organizations. I have previously volunteered with ICAAD, New York Cares and am an active member of Women in Machine Learning and Data Science. I also like to travel (contigent on visa haha) and consider myself an amateur photographer!
