I am a first second thirddel> year PhD student, co-advised by Professor Greg Durrett and Professor Jessy Li. I have recently moved to NYU to continue my PhD!

I started my PhD at UT Austin, where I was a part of the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning (TAUR) group, at the University of Texas at Austin as well as the wider UT NLP group. I am interested in understanding how LLMs perform at creative tasks and how we can leverage reasoning and iterative editing with natural language feedback as mechanisms to make model responses more creative!

Experience

Previously, I was an Associate Research Engineer with the R&D team at Goldman Sachs for three years. This team was led by Vijay Saraswat. I closely worked with Johannes Hoffart and Luciano Del Corro on problems in the space of Document AI and Conversation Understanding specific to the field of Finance.

I completed my Master's in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University where I worked with Professor Mark Dredze. I did my undergrad from IIIT-Delhi where I majored in Computer Science.
