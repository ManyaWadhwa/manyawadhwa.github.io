Manya Wadhwa

I am currently a PhD student at the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning (TAUR) group, at the University of Texas at Austin, led by Professor Greg Durrett.

Previously, I was an Associate Research Engineer with the R&D team at Goldman Sachs for three years. This team was led by Vijay Saraswat. I worked on open problems in the space of Document AI and Conversation Understanding specific to the field of Finance.

I completed my Master's in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University where I worked with Professor Mark Dredze in an intersection of NLP and Regulatory Document Understanding. I did my undergrad from IIIT-Delhi where I majored in Computer Science.

Beyond the research world, I like to volunteer my free time and work with non-profit organizations. I have previously volunteered with ICAAD, New York Cares and am an active member of Women in Machine Learning and Data Science. I also like to travel (contigent on visa haha) and consider myself an amateur photographer!
