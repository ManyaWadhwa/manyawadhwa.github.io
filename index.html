Manya Wadhwa

I am currently a PhD student at the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning (TAUR) group, at the University of Texas at Austin, led by Professor Greg Durrett.

Previously, I was an Associate Research Engineer with the R&D team at Goldman Sachs for three years. This team was led by Vijay Saraswat and I also had the opportunity to work with great mentors like Johannes Hoffart and Luciano Del Corro . My work involved using Natural Language Understanding techniques in the space of Finance. We focused mostly on Financial and Regulation Document Understanding. I also dabbled a little in Dialogue Understanding specific to the financial domain. There were a range of NLP tasks I worked on - Information Extraction, Entity Linking, Question Answering and Slot Filling.

I did my Master's from Johns Hopkins University where I worked with Professor Mark Dredze on an intersection on NLP and Public Health.

I did my undergrad from IIIT-Delhi where I majored in Computer Science.

Beyond the research world, I like to volunteer my free time and work with non-profit organizations. I have previously volunteered with ICAAD, New York Cares and am an active member of Women in Machine Learning and Data Science. I also like to travel (contigent on visa haha) and consider myself an amateur photographer! During quarantine I also started actively following Formula 1 and passionately cheering for Ferrari this season (this might be lost cause though)!
