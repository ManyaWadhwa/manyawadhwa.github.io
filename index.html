I am a first second year PhD student at the Text Analysis, Understanding, and Reasoning (TAUR) group, at the University of Texas at Austin, led by Professor Greg Durrett. I am also a part of the UT NLP group. I am interested in the use of natural language feedback for understanding human preferences for better evaluation techniques as well as for improving text generation systems.

Experience

Previously, I was an Associate Research Engineer with the R&D team at Goldman Sachs for three years. This team was led by Vijay Saraswat. I closely worked with Johannes Hoffart and Luciano Del Corro on problems in the space of Document AI and Conversation Understanding specific to the field of Finance.

I completed my Master's in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University where I worked with Professor Mark Dredze in Regulatory Document Understanding. I did my undergrad from IIIT-Delhi where I majored in Computer Science.

I am a supporter of minority in tech, if you are looking for guidance on how to navigate the industry/academia, feel free to reach out on linkedin/email and we can schedule a chat! :)
